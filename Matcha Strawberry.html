"Easy Strawberry Matcha Latte Recipe (Simple Steps)"

Bahan-bahan:

  1. Small saucepan
  2. Small bowl
  3. Whisk
  4. High-speed or immersion blender
  5. ½ cup white sugar (Strawberry Puree)
  6. ½ cup water (Strawberry Puree)
  7. 1 cup fresh strawberries sliced (Strawberry Puree)
  8. 1 teaspoon matcha powder Strawberry Matcha Latte
  9. ⅓ cup hot water Strawberry Matcha Latte
  10. ⅓ cup milk any kind Strawberry Matcha Latte
  11. 1 cup ice Strawberry Matcha Latte

Cara-cara penyediaan:

    1. Pour the sugar, water, and strawberries into a small saucepan. Heat over medium-low, stirring occasionally, until small bubbles form..

    2. Simmer the strawberry mixture for 13 minutes. Then remove from the heat and allow to cool.

    3. While the strawberries are cooking, combine the hot water and matcha powder in a small bowl. Whisk until the matcha is fully dissolved.

    4. Puree the strawberry mixture using a high-speed blender or immersion blender.

    5. Fill your tall glass with ice. Then add 2 tablespoons of your strawberry puree.

    6. Pour the milk on top of the ice and strawberry puree. Then carefully add the matcha. Take a moment to enjoy your beautiful drink!
